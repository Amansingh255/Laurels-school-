Reel-O-Vation (PRISMATIC) 2024

The talented members of the DIGEX Club secured the prestigious Second Position in the Reel-O-Vation (PRISMATIC) 2024 competition held at Tagore International School. This event celebrated creativity and innovation in media, allowing students to express their ideas through short films and multimedia presentations.

The competition was fierce, but our students shone brightly with their unique perspectives and storytelling skills. We are incredibly proud of their hard work and dedication!

A Reel-O-Vation Sensation: Laurel's School Shines Bright

A Cinematic Extravaganza


Reel-O-Vation 2024, a vibrant celebration of cinematic creativity, unfolded its magic at the prestigious Laurel's School, Indore. This year's event was a testament to the burgeoning talent and innovative spirit of young filmmakers.

A Kaleidoscope of Cinematic Brilliance


From captivating short films to mesmerizing music videos, every entry was a masterpiece that showcased the power of storytelling through the lens. The young filmmakers, armed with their cameras and imaginations, brought to life stories that evoked emotions, challenged perspectives, and inspired audiences.

A Triumphant Showcase
Laurel's School emerged as a dominant force, capturing numerous accolades and winning the hearts of the judges. The school's talented students not only excelled in individual categories but also showcased exceptional teamwork in collaborative projects. Their dedication, creativity, and technical prowess were instrumental in their resounding success.

A Legacy of Cinematic Excellence
Reel-O-Vation 2024 was more than just a competition; it was a cultural extravaganza that celebrated the fusion of art and technology. It was a platform where young minds came together to share their passion for filmmaking and inspire one another. Laurel's School, with its commitment to fostering holistic development, once again proved its dedication to nurturing the next generation of filmmakers.
